Weiser*](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/justice-department-intervenes-xai-lawsuit-challenging-colorados-algorithmic-discrimination) — a constitutional challenge to [Colorado's SB 24-205](https://leg.colorado.gov/bills/sb24-205) — placing the federal government's full weight behind an effort to freeze enforcement of the nation's most consequential state AI law.
